Welcome to my astrophysics home page!

I am a second year graduate student, studying astronomy and astrophysics at Harvard. I was at Princeton for undergrad, where I also majored in astrophysics.

My main scientific focus is on the formation and evolution of galaxies. In particular, I'm studying the merger history of galaxies. Using numerical simulations of galaxy evolution, I investigate which observable properties of a galaxy are reliable indicators of its merger history.

I completed several research projects as an undergraduate. My Research page (link to the left) has descriptions of my past projects, including any reports, posters, or publications that came out of each project. My CV page contains an outline of my academic history to date.

I am more than happy to talk to anyone who is interested in my work! Please feel free to contact me by email, or follow me on Twitter.
