Welcome to my astrophysics home page!

I am a first year graduate student, studying astronomy and astrophysics at Harvard. I was at Princeton for undergrad, where I also majored in astrophysics.

My main scientific interests are in extragalactic astronomy and cosmology, which is the study of the history, evolution, and makeup of the universe as a whole. I'm curious about the nature of dark matter and dark energy, the formation and growth of galaxies and clusters, and the epoch of reionization..

I have completed several research projects as an undergraduate (not all of them in cosmology). My Research page (link to the left) has descriptions of my past projects, including any reports, posters, or publications that came out of each project. My CV page contains an outline of my academic history to date.

I am more than happy to talk to anyone who is interested in my work! Please feel free to contact me by email, or follow me on Twitter.
